This domain has been seized

As part of a coordinated international law enforcement action known as Operation Endgame, this domain has been seized by authorities from the United States Department of Justice, Federal Bureau of Investigation, and Europol in accordance with a seizure warrant issued by a United States District Court.

This action was taken against criminal infrastructure involved in the distribution of malware and operation of botnets such as IcedID, Trickbot, SystemBC, Bumblebee, and others.

If you believe you have been affected or have information relevant to this case, please contact your local cybercrime unit or visit Europol or FBI.gov for more details.
